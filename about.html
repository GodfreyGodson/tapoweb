About Tapo

Tanzania Alliance People Organization (TAPO) is a local NGO registered since 7th March 2012 under the Tanzania NGO Act of 2002 with the registration number 00NGO/0005341

TAPO was born as a result of pioneering efforts of its founder members who have long-term technical experience and management in different spheres. The group’s board made up with well experienced professionals in social work practice, public health, legal services, accountant and human resource management. Their combined understanding of local and global guidelines with past experiences working in management and leadership positions has enabled the compilation of organizational policies in line with the needs of the organizations TAPO is governed by Tanzanian legal constitution, a Board of Directors and a project management team led by Director who is in control of TAPO’s implementations. The Director is expertise on Legal service including Human rights, Family law and Probate, as a Registered Advocate, LLM holder and project management hence leading to the fruitfully implementation of its project. Day to day operations are guided by procedure manuals: office policy and procedure manual, Finance policy and operations and project monitoring and evaluation manuals.
